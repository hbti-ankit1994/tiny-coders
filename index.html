Hi there! I'm Byte the Robot! I need YOUR help to learn new things!

📋 Chapter 2: What Are Instructions?
Byte wants to make a sandwich! 🥪 But without clear instructions...
🍞😵
"Put bread"
Puts bread on head!
🥜💥
"Add peanut butter"
Throws the whole jar!
🤖😢
"Done!"
What a mess!
With Good Instructions ✅
1
Pick up two slices of bread 🍞🍞
2
Put them flat on the plate 🍽️
3
Spread peanut butter on one slice 🥜
4
Put the other slice on top! 🥪 🎉
Computers need very clear step-by-step instructions. This is called an Algorithm! 🧠
